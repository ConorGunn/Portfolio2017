"Call Me Mal" Script

Call Me Mal is a 2nd year short film group project about an individual whom is diagnosed with testicular cancer designed to educate young adults who may feel as though this kind of thing cannot happen to them. In this project, I was the lead writer and voice of "Mal".

Say Something Music Video

Shotgun Music Video Edit

This was a lab exercise during my film class in college. We were each given a folder of footage, that being the two people in the car, and told to edit the footage to the song ‘Shotgun’ by Kevin Herm Connolly. However, we were told to only edit the first minute, or two, of the song and I misunderstood and edited the entire song.
